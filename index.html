About Us

The Harvard Undergraduate Cybersecurity Club is a student-run organization at Harvard College dedicated to encouraging exploration of, and raising interest in, cybersecurity. We meet once a week to practice our skills and learn new ones, and every year we participate in cybersecurity competitions. Ok, so there needs to be more stuff here...
